Aggregate

Presolve aggregation

Type: int
Default value: 1
Minimum value: 0
Maximum value: 1

Enables or disables aggregation in presolve. In rare instances, aggregation can lead to an accumulation of numerical errors. Turning it off can sometimes improve solution accuracy.
